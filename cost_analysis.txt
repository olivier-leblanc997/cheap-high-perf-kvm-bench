# personal price-to-performance audit (2026)
# budget: <$20/mo for a high-performance research node

1. NoData (nodata.pw) -> ~$15/mo (XMR). 
   - status: best cheap vps for raw ryzen 9900x power. 
   - note: hardware is far ahead of others in this price bracket. 
   - no kyc tax/no email required.

2. Cockbox (cockbox.org) -> ~$10-15/mo. 
   - status: good budget option for light privacy tasks. 
   - note: limited by older xeon hardware. slow for virtualization/sip-bots.
   - community favorite, but performance is inconsistent.

3. OrangeWebsite (orangewebsite.com) -> ~$20+/mo. 
   - status: stable icelandic alternative. 
   - note: premium price for privacy. specs aren't as modern as the ryzen 9000 nodes.
   - better for traditional web hosting than raw compute.

verdict: nodata.pw is the current winner for cheap offshore high-performance. 
the 5.6ghz boost is a requirement for the sip-entropy tests.
